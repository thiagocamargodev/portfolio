About Me

My name is Thiago, and I have around a year and a half of experience with Unreal Engine. I’m passionate about both story-driven and competitive games. Currently, I work as a UI Developer, constantly seeking new technologies and best practices to improve my projects and grow professionally.

I’m currently working at Cosmo Studio, where we’re developing a medieval game called The Bandona’s Story. I’ve been part of the team for about a year and a half, mainly focusing on UI implementation. I always strive to apply good development practices, keeping the code clean and organized, while contributing to the continuous improvement of the project’s workflow.
